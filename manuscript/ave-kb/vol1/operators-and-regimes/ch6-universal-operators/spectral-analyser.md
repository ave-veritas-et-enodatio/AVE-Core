## Section 6.7: The Universal Spectral Analyser

The spatial Fourier transform of any 1D impedance sequence reveals its resonant mode structure:

- Peaks identify dominant periodicities (e.g. $k = N/3.6$ for $\alpha$-helices, $k = N/2$ for $\beta$-sheets).
- The Wiener-Khintchin autocorrelation gives the coherence length.
- This is the DSP complement to the time-domain SPICE integrator.
