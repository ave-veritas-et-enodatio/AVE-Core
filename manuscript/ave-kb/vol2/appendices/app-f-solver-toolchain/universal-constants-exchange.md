## Universal Constants as Currency Exchange

The fundamental constants $(G, c, \hbar, \varepsilon_0, e)$ are not parameters of nature --- they are **exchange rates** between representational domains:

| Conversion | Exchange Rate | Direction | Physical Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| Length $\leftrightarrow$ Time | $c$ | m $\to$ s | Propagation speed |
| Mass $\leftrightarrow$ Length | $G/c^2$ | kg $\to$ m | Gravitational "charge radius" |
| Energy $\leftrightarrow$ Length | $\hbar c$ | J $\to$ m | Quantum wavelength |
| Length $\leftrightarrow$ Charge | $\xi_{\mathrm{topo}} = e / l_{\mathrm{node}}$ | m $\to$ C | Topological dislocation |
| Current $\leftrightarrow$ Field | $\varepsilon_0\,c$ | A $\to$ V/m | Displacement current |
| Energy $\leftrightarrow$ Temperature | $k_B$ | J $\to$ K | Thermal excitation |

The AVE framework has **zero free parameters** precisely because all physical quantities reduce to geometric ratios of the lattice pitch $l_{\mathrm{node}}$ through these exchange rates.
