## Derivation: $g_* = 85.75$

The effective relativistic degrees of freedom $g_*$ is **derived from** $\nu_{vac} = 2/7$:

- The Poisson ratio $\nu_{vac} = 2/7$ reveals that each lattice node has **7 independent compliance modes** (the denominator).
- In 3D: $7^3 = 343$ total modes (7 per spatial dimension, cubed).
- The K4 unit cell has 4 nodes, so the effective DOF per cell is:

> **[Resultbox]** *Effective Degrees of Freedom*
>
> $$
> g_* = \frac{7^3}{4} = \frac{343}{4} = 85.75
> $$
