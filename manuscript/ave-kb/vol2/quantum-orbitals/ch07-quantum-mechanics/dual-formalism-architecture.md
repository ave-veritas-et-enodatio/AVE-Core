## E2 Summary --- Dual-Formalism Architecture

> **[Resultbox]** *E2 Summary --- Dual-Formalism Architecture*
>
> AVE provides two complementary solver formalisms, both scale-invariant:
>
> | | **Y$\to$S** | **ABCD cascade** |
> |---|---|---|
> | Topology | Lumped nodes (Hopf links) | Distributed graded line |
> | Eigenvalue | $\lambda_{\min}(S^\dagger S) = 0$ | $B_{\text{total}}(E) = 0$ |
> | EE analog | SPICE netlist | Cascaded TL sections |
> | Problem | P2: same-shell pairs | P3: cross-shell screening |
>
> Both are needed for multi-electron atoms:
>
> 1. **ABCD** $\to$ radial eigenvalue through screened potential (5.58 eV for Li).
> 2. **Y$\to$S** $\to$ Op2 crossing correction from Hopf topology ($-0.26$ eV).
>
> **Result**: IE $= 5.32$ eV vs. 5.39 eV experimental (1.2%, 0 free parameters).
