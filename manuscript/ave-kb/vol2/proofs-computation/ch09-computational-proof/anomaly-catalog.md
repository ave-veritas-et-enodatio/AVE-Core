## Anomaly Catalog: Proposed Tests

The following anomalies and phenomena are identified as targets for future AVE verification:

### Tier 2: Precision Tests

1. **Flyby anomaly:** Unexplained $\Delta v$ of $\sim$3--13 mm/s during Earth flybys. Predict: impedance gradient at magnetopause produces differential phase shift.
2. **Muon $g-2$:** $\Delta a_\mu = 2.49 \times 10^{-9}$. AVE predicts a vacuum self-energy correction from lattice topology.
3. **Hubble tension:** $H_0 = 67$ (CMB) vs 73 (Cepheid). AVE's $H_\infty$ is a single number; scale-dependent impedance may resolve the tension.
4. **Proton radius puzzle:** Already solved --- $d_p = 4\hbar/m_p c \approx 0.841$ fm matches muonic hydrogen.

### Tier 3: Deep Predictions

1. **Fast radio bursts:** Dispersion measure via $Z_{plasma}(r)$ integration along line of sight.
2. **Cosmic ray knee/ankle:** Impedance-driven GZK limit from lattice saturation at $V/V_{snap} \sim 1$.
3. **Pioneer anomaly:** Solar wind drag vs thermal radiation pressure on the RTG.
4. **Saturn ring system:** Ring gaps as impedance waveguide modes (cf. Kirkwood gaps).
5. **Enceladus plume:** Saturn's Io analog --- tidal heating $\to$ cryovolcanism $\to$ plasma source.

### Tier 4: Hardware Verification

1. **Chiral Antennas:** Wire-stitched impedance antenna measuring vacuum $Z_0$ directly.
2. **High-Voltage Dielectric Rectification:** Asymmetrical solid-state capacitor in mineral oil bath for $\varepsilon_r$ sweep.
3. **Casimir measurement:** Impedance between parallel plates as function of separation.
