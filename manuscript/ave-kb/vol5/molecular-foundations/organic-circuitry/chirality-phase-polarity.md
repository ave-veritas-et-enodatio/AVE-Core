## Chirality as Phase Polarity

Biological life almost exclusively utilizes L-amino acids. In AVE circuit theory, chirality dictates the **physical winding direction** of the core inductor sequence. A left-handed (L) string guarantees a $+90^\circ$ intrinsic phase difference (current leads voltage), locking the biology to a continuous resonant polarity that prevents destructive interference along extended peptide chains.
