# Regime Classifications in Virtual Media

Virtual nodal strain is normalized as $r = A_j / A_c$. The three canonical regime boundaries apply to the dynamic activation field traversing the manifold (via the SiLU gate), not to the static weight structure (Chapter 8, Section 8.6):

- **Regime I $\rightarrow$ II Boundary ($r_I \approx 0.121$)**: The vacuum small-signal limit. Activations under this limit operate in the linear passband.
- **Regime II $\rightarrow$ III Boundary ($r_{II} \approx 0.866$)**: The avalanche threshold. Activations entering this state transition from linear processing to nonlinear saturation ($Q \geq 2$).
- **Regime III $\rightarrow$ IV Boundary ($r_{III} = 1.0$)**: The universal yield limit. The SiLU gate saturates to zero.
