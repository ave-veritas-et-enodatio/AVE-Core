# Regime Boundary Correspondences

The three canonical AVE regime boundaries map to specific pre-activation values via $x = \text{logit}(\sigma) = \ln(\sigma / (1-\sigma))$:

| **AVE Regime** | **$r$** | **$\sigma(x)$** | **$x$** |
|---|---|---|---|
| I $\to$ II | $\sqrt{2\alpha} \approx 0.121$ | 0.993 | $+4.91$ |
| II $\to$ III | $\sqrt{3}/2 \approx 0.866$ | 0.500 | $\mathbf{0.000}$ |
| III $\to$ IV | 1.000 | 0.000 | $-\infty$ |
