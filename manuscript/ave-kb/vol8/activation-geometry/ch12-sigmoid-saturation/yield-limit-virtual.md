# The Yield Limit

The Regime III $\to$ IV boundary ($r = 1.0$) maps to $x \to -\infty$, where $\sigma \to 0$. This corresponds to total signal suppression---the neuron is "buckled" and contributes nothing. In practice, neurons with $x < -4$ have $\sigma < 0.018$, contributing less than 2% of their capacity.
