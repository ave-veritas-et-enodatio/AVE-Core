# The First-Principles Correction: Global $A_c$

Axiom 1 (Spacetime as a cascaded LC network) stringently requires that the "vacuum" is a continuum. A transmission line does not dynamically redefine its intrinsic $Z_0$ or yield stress at every spatial interval. As such, the thermodynamic baseline must be a unified, global property of the entire topological medium.

Therefore, the yield limit $A_c$ must be calculated by sampling $A^2$ across the **entire network architecture**:

$$
A_c^2 = \frac{1}{N_{total}} \sum_{L=1}^{Layers} \sum_{j=1}^{N_L} A_{L,j}^2
$$
