> **[Resultbox]** *The Ideal Gas Law*
>
> $$
> PV = nRT
> $$
>
> Where $P$ is Pressure, $V$ is Volume, $n$ is the amount of substance (moles), $R$ is the ideal gas constant, and $T$ is the absolute temperature.
