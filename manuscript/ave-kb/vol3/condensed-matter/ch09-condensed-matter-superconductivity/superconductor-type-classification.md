> **[Resultbox]** *Superconductor Type Classification*
>
> The Ginzburg-Landau parameter $\kappa = \lambda_L / \xi_0$ classifies the saturation geometry:
>
> $$\kappa < \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}: \quad \text{Type I (uniform } \mu \to 0\text{)}, \qquad \kappa > \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}: \quad \text{Type II (vortex lattice)}$$
>
> In the AVE framework:
>
> - **Type I** = Uniform saturation: $\mu_{eff} \to 0$ homogeneously throughout the bulk. The entire volume enters Regime IV simultaneously.
> - **Type II** = Localized saturation vortices: $\mu_{eff} \to 0$ only within quantized flux-tube cores (Abrikosov lattice), while the inter-vortex regions remain in Regime I.
