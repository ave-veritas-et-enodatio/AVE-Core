## Verification: Baryon Asymmetry

The value $g_* = 85.75$ can be independently verified against the observed baryon-to-photon ratio $\eta$. Substituting into the baryon asymmetry formula with all other factors derived from AVE constants:

> **[Resultbox]** *The Baryon Asymmetry*
>
> $$\eta = \frac{(\pi / \kappa_{FS}) \cdot \alpha_W^4 \cdot C_{sph}}{g_*} = \frac{(\pi / 8\pi) \cdot \alpha_W^4 \cdot (28/79)}{85.75} = 6.08 \times 10^{-10}$$

The observed value is $\eta_{obs} = 6.1 \times 10^{-10}$ (WMAP/Planck). **Error: 0.38%.** Using the Standard Model value $g_{*,SM} = 106.75$ yields 20% error.
