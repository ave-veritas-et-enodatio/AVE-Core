## The Arrow of Time

Therefore, the "Arrow of Time" is not a time-dimension mystery; it is simply the deterministic, geometric reality that spherical wave radiation across a Cartesian grid is effectively a one-way street. Thermodynamics is fully resolved as Continuum Fluid Mechanics.
