> **[Resultbox]** *Vacuum Poisson Ratio*
>
> $$
> \nu_{vac} = \frac{3K_{vac} - 2G_{vac}}{2(3K_{vac} + G_{vac})} = \frac{6G_{vac} - 2G_{vac}}{2(6G_{vac} + G_{vac})} = \frac{4}{14} = \frac{2}{7}
> $$
