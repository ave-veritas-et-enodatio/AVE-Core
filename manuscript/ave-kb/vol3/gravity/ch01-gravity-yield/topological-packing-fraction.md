> **[Resultbox]** *Topological Packing Fraction*
>
> $$
> p^* = \frac{10 z_0 - 12}{z_0(z_0 + 2)} = 8\pi\alpha \approx 0.1834
> $$

where $z_0 \approx 51.25$ is the effective coordination number of the chiral amorphous (disordered, non-crystalline) lattice. Non-integer coordination is generic for amorphous networks; integer coordination is a crystalline feature. At this coordination, $p_G = 6/z_0 \approx 0.117$, and the vacuum operates $56.7\%$ above the fluid-solid transition---a robust structural margin.
