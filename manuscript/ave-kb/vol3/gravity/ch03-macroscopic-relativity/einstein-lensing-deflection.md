## Verification: The Einstein Lensing Deflection

To falsify this Optical Metric, a numerical ray-tracing simulation of a photon passing the Sun. Integrating Snell's Law through this specific refractive gradient yields a total deflection angle of:

> **[Resultbox]** *Einstein Lensing Deflection*
>
> $$
> \delta = \frac{4GM}{b c^2}
> $$

This result reproduces the Einstein prediction, distinguishing the AVE framework from Newtonian corpuscular models ($\delta = 2GM/bc^2$) without invoking higher-dimensional curvature.
