> **[Resultbox]** *Gordon Optical Metric*

> -\left(\frac{c^{4}}{7G}\right)\nabla^{2}\epsilon_{11}(r) = 4\pi Mc^{2}\delta^{3}(r)

> **[Resultbox]** *Radial Strain to Newtonian Potential*

> $$
> \epsilon_{11}(r) = \frac{7GM}{c^{2}r}
> $$
