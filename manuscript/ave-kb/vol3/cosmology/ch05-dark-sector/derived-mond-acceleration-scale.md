## Derived MOND Acceleration Scale ($a_0$)

The most dramatic application of scale invariance closes the "dark matter problem." The MOND acceleration scale $a_0 \approx 1.2 \times 10^{-10}$ m/s$^2$ is first *derived*:

> **[Resultbox]** *Derived MOND Acceleration Scale ($a_0$)*
>
> $$
> a_0 = \frac{c \cdot H_\infty}{2\pi} \approx 1.07 \times 10^{-10}\;\text{m/s}^2
> $$

<!-- label: eq:a0_derived -->

where $H_\infty$ is the asymptotic Hubble parameter from the derivation chain (10.7% below the empirical value).
