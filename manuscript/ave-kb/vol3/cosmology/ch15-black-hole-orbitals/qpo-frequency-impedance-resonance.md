## QPO Frequency from Impedance Resonance

> **[Resultbox]** *QPO Frequency from Impedance Resonance*
>
> $$
> \nu_n = \frac{1}{2\pi} \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r_n^3}}
> $$

For GRS 1915+105 ($M \approx 14\,M_\odot$, $a_* \approx 0.7$), the impedance resonance solver predicts the fundamental QPO mode at $\nu_1 \approx 77$ Hz. The observed fundamental QPO frequency is $67$ Hz---a $15\%$ first-principles prediction from the impedance topology. Zero parameterisation: the only inputs are the observationally measured mass $M$ and spin $a_*$ of the target object.
