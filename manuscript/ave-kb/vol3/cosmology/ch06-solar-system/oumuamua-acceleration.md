## 'Oumuamua (1I/2017 U1)

<!-- label: sec:oumuamua -->

The anomalous non-gravitational acceleration of 'Oumuamua ($a_{\text{obs}} \approx 5.0 \times 10^{-6}$ m/s$^2$ at 1 AU, scaling as $1/r^2$) is naturally explained by solar radiation pressure on a high area-to-mass body:

> **[Resultbox]** *'Oumuamua Non-Gravitational Acceleration*
>
> $$
> a_{\text{rad}} = \frac{L_\odot}{4\pi r^2 c} \cdot \frac{A}{m} \approx 4.54 \times 10^{-6}\;\text{m/s}^2 \quad (A/m \approx 1\;\text{m}^2/\text{kg})
> $$

<!-- label: eq:oumuamua_accel -->

This gives a 91% match with zero free parameters.
