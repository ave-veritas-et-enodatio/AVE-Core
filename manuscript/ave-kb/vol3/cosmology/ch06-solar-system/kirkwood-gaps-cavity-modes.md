## Kirkwood Gaps as Cavity Modes

<!-- label: sec:kirkwood -->

The asteroid belt gaps at mean-motion resonances with Jupiter are modelled as cavity modes in the gravitational impedance field: $a_{\text{gap}} = a_J \cdot (q/p)^{2/3}$. All five major gaps (4:1, 3:1, 5:2, 7:3, 2:1) are reproduced to $< 0.3\%$ error.
