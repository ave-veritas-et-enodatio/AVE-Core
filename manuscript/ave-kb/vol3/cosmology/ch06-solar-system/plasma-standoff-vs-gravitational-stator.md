# The Plasma Standoff vs. The Gravitational Stator

A common pedagogical risk is conflating the physical solar plasma (which interacts with the magnetosphere) with the macroscopic orbital coupling (which interacts with the gravitational LC metric). The AVE Topo-Kinematic framework explicitly decouples these "Two Winds" emitted by the Sun:

1. **The Plasma Wind:** The Solar Wind is a physical, violent stream of electrically charged plasma. It couples electromagnetically to the planetary magnetopause. The planetary magnetosphere acts purely as an acoustic electromagnetic shock absorber standing off the kinetic plasma.

2. **The Gravitational Metric:** Gravity is structurally the background scalar tension of the LC lattice. The Sun's standing orbital acoustic cavities completely ignore the planetary magnetic envelope. Instead, orbital reactive power ($VARs$) couples robustly to the sheer volumetric density of structural topological nodes: the bulk physical mass of the planet. In a macroscopic AC Motor analogy, the dense atomic lattice of the planet acts as the "Gravitational Stator" perfectly locking into the Sun's LC phase-rotation. ## Venus and Mars as the Control Group

This structural separation is proven definitively by Venus and Mars. Neither planet possesses an active intrinsic dynamo (no global magnetosphere), and thus both suffer aggressive atmospheric stripping precisely because they lack a plasma impedance cavity. Yet, their orbits obey identically precise Topo-Kinematic Keplerian mechanics as Earth and Jupiter. They suffer zero anomalous gravitational friction because the gravitational AC stator anchors exclusively to the atomic mass lattice, operating wholly independent of the magnetic planetary boundaries.
