## Standing Wave Enhancement

At the magnetopause, the reflected solar wind superposes with the incident field. By transmission line theory:

> **[Resultbox]** *Chapman-Ferraro Boundary Enhancement*
>
> $$
> B_{eff} = B_{dipole} \times (1 + |\Gamma|)
> $$

For a highly reflective boundary, $B_{eff} \to 2 B_{dipole}$, giving $4\times$ pressure enhancement. This is the Chapman-Ferraro result derived from first principles of impedance matching.
