## Heliospheric Impedance Profile

<!-- label: sec:solar_impedance -->

The solar wind creates a radial impedance profile extending from the photosphere to the interstellar medium:

> **[Resultbox]** *Heliospheric Impedance Profile*
>
> $$
> Z_{sw}(r) = \frac{Z_0}{\sqrt{1 - (\omega_p(r)/\omega)^2}}, \quad n_e(r) = n_0 \left(\frac{1\;\text{AU}}{r}\right)^2
> $$

<!-- label: eq:heliospheric_Z -->

The heliopause at $\sim$120 AU acts as an impedance boundary where $n_e$ drops sharply to interstellar values.
