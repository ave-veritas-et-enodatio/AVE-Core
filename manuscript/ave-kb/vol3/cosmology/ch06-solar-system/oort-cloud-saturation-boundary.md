## Oort Cloud as Saturation Boundary

<!-- label: sec:oort_cloud -->

The inner boundary of the Oort Cloud corresponds to the radius where solar gravitational acceleration reaches the MOND threshold:

> **[Resultbox]** *Oort Cloud Saturation Boundary*
>
> $$
> g_N(r) = \frac{GM_\odot}{r^2} = a_0 \quad\longrightarrow\quad r_{\text{sat}} = \sqrt{\frac{GM_\odot}{a_0}} \approx 7{,}400\;\text{AU}
> $$

<!-- label: eq:oort_saturation -->

This falls within the observed Hills Cloud range (2,000--5,000 AU inner edge), confirming that the Oort Cloud is the solar system's *impedance boundary*.
