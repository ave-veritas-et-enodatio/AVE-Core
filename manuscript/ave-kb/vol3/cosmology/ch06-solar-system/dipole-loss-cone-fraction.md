## Internal Plasma Pressure

Trapped magnetospheric particles contribute pressure via the adiabatic invariant. The trapped fraction is derived from the dipole loss cone:

> **[Resultbox]** *Dipole Loss Cone Trapped Fraction*
>
> $$
> f_{trap} = 1 - \sqrt{1 - (R_p/R_{mp})^3}
> $$
