## Macroscopic Avalanche Transconductance

> **[Resultbox]** *Macroscopic Avalanche Transconductance*
>

> I(V) = I_S \left( e^{\frac{V}{V_T}} - 1 \right) \times \left( \frac{1}{1 - \left(\frac{V}{V_{bd}}\right)^\alpha} \right)
