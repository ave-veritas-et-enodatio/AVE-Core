## Mass Prediction Accuracy

[Figure: mass_error_vs_Z.png --- see manuscript/vol_6_periodic_table/figures/]

**AVE Mass Prediction Accuracy vs. Atomic Number.** Green markers indicate exact semiconductor engine solutions ($0.000\%$ error). Orange: Large Signal (avalanche) regime. Cyan: sub-$0.1\%$ Fibonacci packing solutions. Yellow: $0.1$--$0.5\%$ residual. Red: $>0.5\%$ error, pending re-solution with resolved geometry.

Exact Large Signal solutions ($0.000\%$ error) are found for S-32 ($M=32.8$, $R=4.66d$) and Ca-40 ($M=32.9$, $R=5.86d$). Near-exact Small Signal solutions ($<0.001\%$) are found for Ar-40, Ti-48, Cr-52, and Fe-56 using Platonic/Archimedean packing geometries. The remaining elements use Fibonacci lattice packing as a geometric proxy and carry residual errors typically below $0.5\%$.
