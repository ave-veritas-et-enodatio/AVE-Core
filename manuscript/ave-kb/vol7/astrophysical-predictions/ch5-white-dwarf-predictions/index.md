# Ch.5: White Dwarf Gravitational Predictions

This chapter applies the six-step AVE methodology to the gravitational environment of white dwarf stars. Two distinct predictions emerge: a saturation correction to the spectral gravitational redshift, and a standing shear wave cavity with eigenfrequencies in the LIGO band. The derivation chain traces each claim from the four fundamental axioms, through the universal operators, to a numerical prediction compared against observation.
