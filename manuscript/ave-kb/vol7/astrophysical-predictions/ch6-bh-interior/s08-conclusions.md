# Conclusions

1. The BH interior is Regime IV of the universal saturation operator:
   $S(\varepsilon_{11}) = 0$ for $r < r_{sat} = 7GM/c^2$.

2. Unlike particle confinement (asymmetric saturation, $\Gamma = -1$),
   the BH has **symmetric** saturation: $Z = Z_0$ everywhere,
   $\Gamma = 0$. It is a dissipative sink, not a resonant cavity.

3. The $0 \cdot \infty$ singularity at $r \to 0$ is the signature
   that the LC model breaks down in the ruptured interior---analogous
   to querying the AC model of a destroyed semiconductor device.

4. In EE terms: the BH interior is the DC operating point
   past dielectric breakdown. The "small-signal model" (GW propagation)
   does not exist because the "device" (lattice topology) is destroyed.

5. The exterior supports shear waves; the QNM eigenfrequencies
   arise from $r_{eff} = r_{sat}/(1+\nu_{vac})$, the same formula
   used for WD surface modes and validated to 1.7% against GR.
