# Step 6: Testability and Predictions

1. **No information paradox.**
   The BH interior is a dissipative sink, not a unitary cavity.
   Information is not "stored"---it is absorbed by the ruptured lattice
   (topology destroyed). This resolves the unitarity debate:
   the question "where does information go?" has the same answer
   as "where does charge go when a capacitor breaks down?"

2. **Hawking radiation = gravitational Schwinger effect.**
   Vacuum fluctuations at $r \approx r_{sat}$ where $G_{shear} \to 0$
   create particle pairs. This is the gravitational analogue of
   Schwinger pair production at $E = E_{yield}$ (same $S \to 0$ boundary,
   different constitutive sector).

3. **BH merger ringdown (validated).**
   The collision of two ruptured regions creates a new $r_{sat}$ boundary.
   The ringdown IS the exterior lattice equilibrating to the new
   saturation shell. Already validated to 1.7% via the 5-step
   eigenvalue method ($\omega \cdot M = 0.3673$ vs. GR exact $0.3737$).
