# RF Topological Routing: Legacy Digital Interfacing

Seamless integration with discrete legacy logic standards demands robust conversion and interference suppression limits. Orthogonal harmonic basis synthesis physically transforms temporal bitstreams into spatial sub-harmonic wave packets that propagate through the native VCA substrate without aliasing or inter-symbol interference.

The converter stage operates at the APU boundary (Ch 21), where SerDes circuitry decomposes incoming digital waveforms into their Fourier constituents, mapping each bit to a distinct spatial frequency channel. The reverse process at the output reconstructs the digital stream from the diffracted output harmonics.
