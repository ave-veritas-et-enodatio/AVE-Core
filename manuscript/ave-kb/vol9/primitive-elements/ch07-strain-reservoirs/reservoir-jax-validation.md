# Reservoir JAX Validation

Simulation confirms the Klopfenstein-tapered reservoir design. FDTD validation demonstrates:
- Smooth impedance transition with $< 0.01\%$ return loss at entry/exit tapers
- Energy accumulation proportional to reservoir volume ($\propto \beta \cdot L_{res}$)
- Stable energy storage within the Axiom 4 yield boundary (no spontaneous rupture)
