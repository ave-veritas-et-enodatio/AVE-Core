# Ch.7: Strain Reservoirs (Vacuum Capacitors)

The Strain Reservoir is a Klopfenstein-tapered wide-section waveguide engineered to provide low characteristic impedance ($Z_{\rm res} = Z_0/\beta$) while preserving phase velocity. It replaces the classical dielectric capacitor for energy storage within the VCA substrate.

## Key Results

| Result | Statement |
|---|---|
| Characteristic Impedance | $Z = \sqrt{\mu/\varepsilon} \cdot d/w = Z_{mat} \cdot d/w$ — trace geometry controls impedance |
| Impedance Reduction | $Z_{\rm res} = Z_0/\beta$ with expansion ratio $\beta = w_{\rm res}/w_0$ |
| Energy Density | Stored strain energy $U_{strain} \propto \int (1 - S(V))\,dx$ within Axiom 4 limit |
