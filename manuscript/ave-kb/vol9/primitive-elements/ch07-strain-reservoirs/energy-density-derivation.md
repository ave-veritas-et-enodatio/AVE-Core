# Energy Density Derivation

The maximum energy stored in a Strain Reservoir is bounded by Axiom 4. The strain energy per unit volume is:

$$u_{strain} = \frac{1}{2}\varepsilon_0 V^2 \cdot (1 - S(V))$$

where $S(V) = \sqrt{1 - (V/V_{snap})^2}$ is the saturation kernel. The total stored energy integrates over the reservoir volume, with energy accumulating as the amplitude approaches — but never exceeding — the $V_{snap}$ yield boundary.
