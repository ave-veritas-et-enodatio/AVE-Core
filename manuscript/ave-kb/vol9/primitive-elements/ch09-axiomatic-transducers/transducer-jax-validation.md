# Transducer JAX Validation

The Klopfenstein taper simulation confirms:
- The impedance profile smoothly transitions from $50\,\Omega$ to $376.73\,\Omega$ over $70\,\mu\text{m}$
- Reflectance drops from the catastrophic 58.7% step value to $< 0.01\%$ ($< -40\,\text{dB}$) across the entire passband above $f_{CC}$
- The VCA waveguide (double-line notation) shows wider line separation in the $50\,\Omega$ external domain, narrowing to standard VCA spacing in the $376.73\,\Omega$ domain
- Zero power dissipation — purely reactive energy transfer
