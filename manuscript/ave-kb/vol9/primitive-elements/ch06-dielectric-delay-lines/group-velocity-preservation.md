# Group Velocity Preservation

Within the passband of the corrugated structure, the dispersion relation remains approximately linear. The group velocity $v_g = d\omega/dk$ matches the phase velocity, ensuring that wavepackets (logic pulses) propagate without temporal spreading. Dispersion becomes significant only near the Bragg stop-band edges where $k \approx \pi/\Lambda$.
