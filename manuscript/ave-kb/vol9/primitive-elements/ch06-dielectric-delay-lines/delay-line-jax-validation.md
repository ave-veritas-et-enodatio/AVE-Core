# Delay Line JAX Validation

Simulation confirms the analytic slow-wave predictions. The corrugated waveguide FDTD solver demonstrates:
- Phase velocity retardation proportional to corrugation depth
- Preserved pulse shape (no dispersion) within the designed passband
- Impedance matching maintained across the corrugated section via Klopfenstein entry/exit tapers
