# Ch.4: Geometric Diodes & Continuous Flow Gates

Derives the continuous Geometric Diode from conservation of energy and Axiom 4 metric saturation. Proves that asymmetric trace geometry produces $\Gamma \to -1$ total reflection under reverse bias, replicating ideal transistor gating without chemical doping.

## Key Results

| Result | Statement |
|---|---|
| Amplitude Scaling | $V_2 = V_1 \sqrt{w_1/w_2}$ — conservation of energy through constriction |
| Forward Bias | $w_2 > w_1 \Rightarrow V_2 < V_1$; wave dilates in linear Regime I, $\Gamma \approx 0$ |
| Reverse Bias | $w_2 < w_1 \Rightarrow V_2 > V_1$; amplitude forced toward $V_{snap}$, $S(V) \to 0$, $Z_{eff} \to \infty$, $\Gamma \to -1$ |
