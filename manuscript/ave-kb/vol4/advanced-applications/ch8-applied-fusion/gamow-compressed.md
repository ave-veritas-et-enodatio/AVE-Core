## Compressed Gamow Exponent

> **[Resultbox]** *Compressed Gamow Exponent*
>
> $$
> \eta(n) = \frac{\eta_0}{n}
> $$

The barrier is not lowered---it is *narrowed*. Since the tunnelling probability depends exponentially on $\eta$, even a modest compression dramatically enhances the cross-section.
