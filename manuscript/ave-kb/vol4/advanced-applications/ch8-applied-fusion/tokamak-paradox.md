## The Tokamak Ignition Paradox (The 60.3 kV Alignment)

The average mechanical force generated during this deceleration evaluates to $F = E_k / d \approx 0.0250$ Newtons.

This reveals a critical theoretical result: **$60.3 \text{ kV} > 43.65 \text{ kV}$ (The Vacuum Dielectric Saturation Yield Limit).**
