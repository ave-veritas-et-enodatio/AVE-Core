# The P-N Junction as an S-Parameter Boundary

The depletion zone and the resulting $I$-$V$ properties emerge deterministically from the impedance step $Z_p \to Z_n$. An AVE-native design engine models the diode as an $S_{11}$ reflection barrier where forward bias simply shifts the transmission coefficient $T^2$ via the topological saturation function.

Silicon's unique placement at the precipice of the avalanche boundary ($V_R/V_{BR} = 0.050$, $M=1.000$) establishes it as the heaviest symmetric alpha-cluster element stable in the bulk linear regime.
