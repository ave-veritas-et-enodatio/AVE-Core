# Ch.6 Sustaining Micro-Newton Torsion Metrology

This chapter establishes the complete measurement protocol, dielectric bath configuration, and artifact rejection criteria required to achieve a definitive, falsifiable thrust measurement. The predicted signal ($\sim 45\;\mu$N for PONDER-01; $\sim 470\;\mu$N for PONDER-05) must be extracted from an environment saturated with electromagnetic interference, thermal gradients, outgassing transients, and electrostatic artifacts.

## Key Results

| Result | Statement |
|---|---|
| Torsion balance deflection | $\theta = F \cdot L \cdot 2l / (\pi G_{wire} r^4)$ |
| Sensitivity | $< 1\;\mu$N with 25 $\mu$m tungsten wire |
| Mineral oil corona margin | $20\times$ safety at 30 kV / 50 mm gap |
| Quartz thermal dissipation | $< 0.001$ mW (indefinite CW) |
| Impedance matching | Oil reduces reflected power from 12.9% to $\sim$3.4% |
| Artifact rejection | Eight-point protocol covering ion wind, thermal, electrostatic, vibration, outgassing, cable, Lorentz, statistical |
