## The Epistemology of Falsification

A scientific framework is only as robust as its capacity to be empirically proven wrong. Theoretical physics over the last century has produced parameterised, abstract mathematical models (e.g., String Theory, M-Theory, Supersymmetry) that have so far evaded experimental falsification by occupying unobservable, trans-Planckian energy regimes.

The Applied Vacuum Engineering (AVE) framework is constructed to be falsifiable. Because it is a **One-Parameter Effective Field Theory** --- where all masses, forces, and cosmological constants are algebraically interlocked and geometrically derived from the single fundamental Planck node calibration limit --- altering or tuning any one output breaks the mathematical framework.

AVE makes falsifiable predictions about the macroscopic and microscopic dynamics of the universe that are testable on tabletop laboratory benches.
