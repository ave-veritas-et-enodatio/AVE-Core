When driven at Very High Frequencies (VHF) across an extreme geometric asymmetry, the non-linear saturation operator ($\varepsilon_{eff}(V)$) mechanically rectifies the strain. This **Non-Linear Stress Rectification** explicitly converts continuous high-frequency vibration into direct, macroscopic unidirectional thrust *against the rigid vacuum metric itself*.
