## Sagnac-RLVG & Metric Telemetry

### Material-Dependent Entrainment Law

AVE rejects the GR claim that Sagnac shift depends only on area and angular velocity. The **Kinematic & Electromagnetic Entrainment Law**: macroscopic metric drag scales with:

1. **Mass density** ($\rho_m$): denser rotors grip vacuum more strongly
2. **Magnetic permeability** ($\mu_r$): high-$\mu$ materials (Iron, Mu-Metal) generate LC vacuum eddy currents
3. **Background EMI** ($B$): external DC fields pre-saturate vacuum inductance
4. **Altitude**: lower gravity = lower ambient metric strain = reduced coupling
5. **Latitude**: Earth's Lense-Thirring drag cross-talks with RLG alignment

**Falsification**: Identical RLGs at same RPM with Aerogel vs Lead, and Aluminum vs Mu-Metal. If Sagnac shift is density/material-independent → AVE killed.
