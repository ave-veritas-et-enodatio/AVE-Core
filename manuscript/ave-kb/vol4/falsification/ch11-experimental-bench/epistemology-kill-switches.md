<!-- claim-quality: om0rtq -->

**1. Neutrino Parity Test**

**2. GRB Dispersion Test**

**3. Birefringence Kill Switch**

High-intensity laser interferometry measuring the $E^2$ vs $E^4$ slope provides an absolute binary discriminator.
