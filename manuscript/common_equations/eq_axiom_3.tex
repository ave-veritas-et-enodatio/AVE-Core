\begin{resultbox}{Axiom 3 — Effective Action Principle}
The continuous system evolves strictly to minimize the macroscopic hardware action $S_{AVE}$. The dynamics are encoded entirely in the continuous phase transport field ($\mathbf{A}$):
\begin{equation}
    \mathcal{L}_{node} = \frac{1}{2}\epsilon_0 |\partial_t \mathbf{A}_n|^2 - \frac{1}{2\mu_0} |\nabla \times \mathbf{A}_n|^2
    \label{eq:axiom3_lagrangian}
\end{equation}
The action principle minimizes $S_{AVE} = \int \mathcal{L}_{node}\, d^4x$ across the network. Equivalently, in operational form: the system evolves to minimize reflection $|\Gamma|^2$ at all internal impedance boundaries (least reflected action / S$_{11}$ minimization). Energy conservation, Lorentz invariance, and gauge symmetry follow as Noether consequences of $\mathcal{L}_{node}$'s symmetry structure.
\end{resultbox}
