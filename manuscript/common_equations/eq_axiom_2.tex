\begin{resultbox}{Axiom 2 — Fine Structure}
The fine-structure constant couples topology to impedance:
\begin{equation}
    \alpha = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0\hbar c} \approx \frac{1}{137.036}
    \label{eq:axiom2_alpha}
\end{equation}
$\alpha$ sets the saturation threshold for the dielectric yield voltage:
\begin{equation}
    V_{yield} = \sqrt{\alpha} \cdot V_{snap} = \sqrt{\alpha} \cdot \frac{m_e c^2}{e} \approx 43.65\;\text{kV}
    \label{eq:axiom2_v_yield}
\end{equation}
\end{resultbox}
