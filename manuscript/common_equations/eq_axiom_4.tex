\begin{resultbox}{Axiom 4 — Universal Saturation Kernel}
The universal yield kernel bounding all LC modes:
\begin{equation}
    S(A) = \sqrt{1 - \left(\frac{A}{A_{yield}}\right)^{\!2}}
    \label{eq:axiom4_saturation}
\end{equation}
At $A = 0$: $S = 1$ (linear Maxwell recovered).\quad
At $A \to A_{yield}$: $S \to 0$ (saturation).

\medskip
\textbf{Derived consequences:}
