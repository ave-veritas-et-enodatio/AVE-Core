\begin{resultbox}{Axiom 1 — Impedance}
The vacuum is a discrete LC resonant network with characteristic impedance
\begin{equation}
    Z_0 = \sqrt{\frac{\mu_0}{\varepsilon_0}} \approx 376.73\;\Omega
    \label{eq:axiom1_impedance}
\end{equation}
and lattice pitch
\begin{equation}
    \ell_{node} = \frac{\hbar}{m_e c} \approx 3.86 \times 10^{-13}\;\text{m}
    \label{eq:axiom1_pitch}
\end{equation}
$\mu_0$ is the per-node inductance (rotational inertia) and $\varepsilon_0$
is the per-node capacitance (elastic compliance).
\end{resultbox}
