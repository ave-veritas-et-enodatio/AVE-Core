## §4 — Expected signal and sensitivity (updated cycle-12)

**Predicted signal rate** per [cycle-12 parametric coupling kernel](../manuscript/ave-kb/vol4/circuit-theory/ch1-vacuum-circuit-analysis/parametric-coupling-kernel.md) at $\kappa_{HPGe} = 0.05$ ceiling (from MAJORANA implicit null):
$$R_{HPGe}^{predicted} = 5.2 \times 10^{-9}\,\text{events/s/kg} \times 9.39\,\text{kg} \times 86400\,\text{s/day} \approx 4.2 \times 10^{-3}\,\text{events/day}$$

Annual signal events: $\approx 1.5 \text{ events/year}$ in the 2-6 keV window at $\kappa_{HPGe} = 0.05$. At $\kappa_{HPGe} = 0.01$ (factor-5 below MAJORANA ceiling): $\approx 0.3 \text{ events/year}$.

**Background estimate** (clean HPGe at LNGS-class depth, unchanged from cycle-9):
- $10^{-3}$ events/keV/kg/day × 4 keV (2-6 keV window) × 9.39 kg × 365 days $\approx 14{,}000$ background events/year
- S/B at $\kappa_{HPGe} = 0.05$: $\approx 10^{-4}$ broadband; line-resolved S/B $\approx 0.01$ at predicted rate

**Statistical sensitivity timeline** (cycle-12 revised):
- 1 year integration: insufficient for detection at $\kappa_{HPGe} \leq 0.05$ ($\sim 1$ event vs $\sim 14{,}000$ background)
- 5 year integration: 1.5σ excess at $\kappa_{HPGe} = 0.05$ in resolution element; would require multi-experiment combination for 3σ
- **Scale-up consideration**: factor-10 mass scale-up (100 kg HPGe array, comparable to MAJORANA Demonstrator scale) gives 15-30 events/year at $\kappa_{HPGe} \in [0.05, 0.1]$ — detectable at 3-5σ in 2-3 years

**Annual modulation phase** (claim c): at cycle-12 predicted rates, modulation amplitude only detectable with $\sim 100\times$ scale-up (LEGEND-class HPGe array) or via combined dataset with COSINE/ANAIS. Phase determination at single-9.39-kg-detector scale is NOT feasible.

**Operational reframe** (cycle-12): this experiment is now best understood as a TEST of κ_quality lattice-dependence and Z-INDEPENDENCE of line position, with rate-magnitude precision DELEGATED to scale-up (100+ kg HPGe array). At 9.39 kg scale, the most-discriminating observable is whether ANY line at 3.728 keV emerges above background after 3-5 years, plus the modulation-PHASE at next-decade-scale (gated on scale-up).

## §5 — Falsification conditions (updated cycle-12)

The experiment cleanly FALSIFIES the AVE parametric coupling kernel framework if any of:

1. **Falsifier A — Line observed at $\kappa_{HPGe} > 0.1$** (e.g., rate $> 10^{-8}$ events/s/kg at 3.728 keV in HPGe): the cycle-12 lattice-dependence prediction is wrong; HPGe should NOT see significantly higher rates than MAJORANA implicit null permits. A line at NaI-class κ ceiling ($\kappa = 1$ giving ~$5 \times 10^{-7}$) would falsify the entire κ_quality framework.

2. **Falsifier B — Line at Ge native K-line energies STRONGER than at 3.728 keV** (e.g., Ge Kα at 9.886 keV stronger than 3.728 keV after background subtraction): the AVE substrate-rate framing is wrong; DAMA signal is plausibly Ca contamination in NaI specifically. This is the Moseley-anti-anchor test.

3. **Falsifier C — Combined HPGe + Sapphire + CsI(Tl) cross-detector pattern inconsistent with single ε_param × κ_quality framework**: if the κ_quality values required to fit observed rates across three different lattices (Ge diamond + Al₂O₃ corundum + CsI rock-salt) don't correlate with materials-science crystal-quality metrics (mosaicity, defect density, dopant uniformity), the κ_quality framework is ad-hoc and fails predictive content.

4. **Falsifier D — Modulation peak at scale-up (100+ kg) shifted from day-of-year ~152**: at sufficient scale, modulation phase observable; if it's perihelion-aligned (day ~3) or null, the CMB-velocity phase-lock claim fails.

The experiment CONFIRMS the AVE parametric coupling kernel framework if:

- Line observed at $3.728 \pm 0.05$ keV with rate consistent with $\kappa_{HPGe} \in [0.01, 0.1]$ (lattice-dependence + no-Tl-coherence-enhancement predicted regime)
- Line absent at Ge native K-energies above background (no atomic-Z dependence for the 3.728 keV line)
- At scale-up: annual modulation peak observed at day-of-year $152 \pm 30$
- κ_HPGe correlates with HPGe-specific materials-science crystal-quality measurements

**Key cycle-12 distinction from cycle-9 falsifier set**: Falsifier A was previously "no line observed" — now reframed as "line observed at HIGHER rate than predicted." A null observation at 9.39 kg is COMPATIBLE with framework (matches MAJORANA constraint $\kappa_{HPGe} < 0.05$); a positive observation at κ much greater than 0.05 is what falsifies.

## §13 — Sapphire (Al₂O₃) cryogenic forward-prediction proposal (NEW cycle-12)

| Property | Sapphire cryogenic | HPGe | NaI(Tl) DAMA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Crystal Q at operating T | ~10⁹ (cryogenic) | ~10⁴ (77 K) | ~10³ (room) |
| Q·δ_C | $4.6 \times 10^7$ (extreme regenerative) | 457 (deep regenerative) | 45.7 (deep regenerative) |
| κ_quality regime | Saturated at 1 (extreme) | ≤ 1 (lattice-mismatch reduces) | ≤ 1 (Tl-coherence enhances) |

**Sapphire cryogenic could see HIGHER rate per kg than DAMA** — by 1-2 OOM if extreme regenerative regime saturates the κ_quality at lattice ceiling.

**Experimental design** (preliminary):
- Detector: 1 kg single-crystal Al₂O₃ (sapphire boule, scintillator-grade or low-background ultra-pure)
- Cryostat: dilution refrigerator at < 100 mK (or pulse tube at 4 K minimum)
- Background: standard low-background at LNGS/SNOLAB/CJPL (HPGe-equivalent infrastructure shareable)
- Readout: TES (transition-edge sensor) bolometer for athermal-phonon detection — sub-keV resolution achievable
- Expected signal at $\kappa_{Sapphire} \sim 1$: $10^{-5}$ events/s/kg × 1 kg × 86400 s/day ≈ 1 event/day = 365 events/year
- Background at TES sapphire bolometer: ~$10^{-4}$-$10^{-3}$ events/keV/kg/day (CRESST-class) → S/B ~ 10⁴ at the predicted rate per resolution element
- **First-result timeline**: 6 months at $\kappa_{Sapphire} = 1$ ceiling; 1 year at $\kappa_{Sapphire} = 0.1$

1. Highest predicted rate (extreme regenerative)
